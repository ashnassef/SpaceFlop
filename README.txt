#--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------#
#  Major Jam theme is isolation. Limitation is no living things.                       #
#                                                                                      #
#  You are the AI of a space ship. The crew is dead at their stations.                 #
#  The fleet has gone ahead and your thrusters are jammed in a vertical position.      #
#  Jostle your dead crewmen to press the correct controls and reset the thrusters.     #
#  Avoid wasting fuel and energy or risk being stranded.                               #
#  Will you be able to rejoin the fleet?                                               #
